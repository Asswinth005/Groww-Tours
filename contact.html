Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about booking, tours, and our services.

How do I book a tour?
You can book directly through our website by selecting your desired tour, date, and group size. Payment is secure and you'll receive instant confirmation.
What is your cancellation policy?
Free cancellation up to 48 hours before the tour. Within 48 hours, 50% refund. No-shows are non-refundable.
How are guides selected?
All guides undergo a rigorous vetting process including interviews, background checks, and trial tours. We only accept 15% of applicants.
Do you offer group discounts?
Yes! Groups of 6+ receive 10% off, 10+ receive 15% off, and 20+ receive 20% off. Contact us for custom group bookings.
Are tours available in other languages?
Many of our guides are multilingual. You can filter tours by language preference during search.
How do I become a guide?
Visit our "Become a Guide" page, fill out the application form, and our team will review your submission within 5-7 business days.
